# Business Insights – Customer Churn

## Observations
- Customers on month-to-month contracts churn more frequently
- New customers (0–1 year tenure) show higher churn
- Customers with higher monthly charges are more likely to leave
- Certain payment methods have higher churn risk

## Business Recommendations
- Encourage long-term contracts with discounts
- Focus retention efforts on first-year customers
- Provide flexible pricing plans
- Improve experience for high-churn payment methods

## Project Scope Note
This project focuses on **descriptive analytics and basic prediction**
to support business decision-making rather than advanced ML.
